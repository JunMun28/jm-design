DESIGN INTENT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Compact ops-review density. Not marketing.
- Every slide has one decision-oriented headline.
- Slide 01 gives scan-level status.
- Slide 02 explains where loss accumulates.
- Slide 03 narrows to the failure mode and tester outlier.
- Slide 04 converts analysis into lot decisions.
- Slide 05 closes with owner actions, timing, and ask.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VISUAL NOTES FOR HTML VERSION
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Restrained light manufacturing-control-room feel.
- KPI cards compact, max 8px radius.
- Use monospaced numerals for yield values.
- Use one accent for risk / target gap.
- Avoid rainbow charts.
- Animate only if presenting live: bars first, then callout/ask.
