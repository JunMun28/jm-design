Gamified Copilot learning pilot.

Core idea

Make Copilot practice visible.

Proposal: approve a small pilot for a mission-based learning platform across Copilot Chat, Teams, Outlook, SharePoint, and PowerPoint.

Why it matters: employees learn faster when training becomes repeated work practice, not passive content.

What makes it credible: success is measured by work artifacts and task quality, not completion alone.
