BRAINSTORM - NOT FINAL DECK

Gamified Copilot Learning Strategy

Core idea

Turn Copilot learning into workplace practice.

Proposal: Create a gamified learning platform that guides employees through practical Copilot missions across Microsoft 365.

Why it matters: Adoption grows when people practice in real Teams, Outlook, SharePoint, PowerPoint, Word, and Excel moments.

What makes it credible: The strategy stays professional: structured capability building, realistic missions, and measurable progress.
