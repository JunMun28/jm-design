# Botanical Garden

A fresh and organic theme featuring vibrant garden-inspired colors for lively presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Fern Green**: `#4a7c59` - Rich natural green
- **Marigold**: `#f9a620` - Bright floral accent
- **Terracotta**: `#b7472a` - Earthy warm tone
- **Cream**: `#f5f3ed` - Soft neutral backgrounds

**Semantic role mapping**: Fern Green=active/emphasis, Marigold=context/de-emphasized, Terracotta=text, Cream=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans
- **If available**: Merriweather Bold for headers, Source Sans 3 for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Terracotta `#b7472a` on Cream `#f5f3ed` | 4.79:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |

This is the only in-palette pair that passes 4.5:1 — all text must use it. Fern Green on Cream is 4.38:1, just under the AA line: headings and large display only, never body text. Marigold on Cream (1.80:1) is graphic fills only. No pair reaches 7:1, so for long body copy on Cream darken the ink toward near-black.

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Garden centers, food presentations, farm-to-table content, botanical brands, natural products.
