# Midnight Galaxy

A dramatic and cosmic theme with deep purples and mystical tones for impactful presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Purple**: `#2b1e3e` - Rich dark base
- **Cosmic Blue**: `#4a4e8f` - Mystical mid-tone
- **Lavender**: `#a490c2` - Soft accent color
- **Silver**: `#e6e6fa` - Light highlights and text

**Semantic role mapping**: Lavender=active/emphasis, Cosmic Blue=context/de-emphasized, Silver=text, Deep Purple=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSans Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans
- **If available**: Poppins Bold for headers, Open Sans for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Silver `#e6e6fa` on Deep Purple `#2b1e3e` | 12.57:1 | AAA (≥7:1) |
| Silver `#e6e6fa` on Cosmic Blue `#4a4e8f` | 6.14:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |
| Lavender `#a490c2` on Deep Purple `#2b1e3e` | 5.41:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |

All other in-palette pairs fall below 4.5:1 — do not use them for text. Cosmic Blue on Deep Purple (2.05:1) is graphic fills only.

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Entertainment industry, gaming presentations, nightlife venues, luxury brands, creative agencies.
