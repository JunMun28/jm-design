# Modern Minimalist

A clean and contemporary theme with a sophisticated grayscale palette for maximum versatility.

## Color Palette

- **Charcoal**: `#36454f` - Primary dark color
- **Slate Gray**: `#708090` - Medium gray for accents
- **Light Gray**: `#d3d3d3` - Backgrounds and dividers
- **White**: `#ffffff` - Text and clean backgrounds

**Semantic role mapping**: Slate Gray=active/emphasis, Light Gray=context/de-emphasized, Charcoal=text, White=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans
- **If available**: Inter Bold for headers, Inter for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Charcoal `#36454f` on White `#ffffff` | 9.90:1 | AAA (≥7:1) |
| Charcoal `#36454f` on Light Gray `#d3d3d3` | 6.61:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |

All other in-palette pairs fall below 4.5:1 — do not use them for text. Slate Gray on White (4.05:1) works for large display or graphic accents only.

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Tech presentations, architecture portfolios, design showcases, modern business proposals, data visualization.
