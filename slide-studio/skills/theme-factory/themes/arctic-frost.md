# Arctic Frost

A cool and crisp winter-inspired theme that conveys clarity, precision, and professionalism.

## Color Palette

- **Ice Blue**: `#d4e4f7` - Light backgrounds and highlights
- **Steel Blue**: `#4a6fa5` - Primary accent color
- **Silver**: `#c0c0c0` - Metallic accent elements
- **Crisp White**: `#fafafa` - Clean backgrounds and text

**Semantic role mapping**: Steel Blue=active/emphasis and text (the only AA-passing ink), Ice Blue=context/de-emphasized, Silver=context (borders/dividers), Crisp White=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans
- **If available**: IBM Plex Sans Bold for headers, IBM Plex Sans for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Steel Blue `#4a6fa5` on Crisp White `#fafafa` | 4.90:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |

This is the only in-palette pair that passes 4.5:1 — all text must use it. No pair reaches 7:1, so for long body copy on the light base, darken Steel Blue or use a near-black neutral. Steel Blue on Ice Blue (3.95:1) works for large display only.

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Healthcare presentations, technology solutions, winter sports, clean tech, pharmaceutical content.
