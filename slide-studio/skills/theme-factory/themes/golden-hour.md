# Golden Hour

A rich and warm autumnal palette that creates an inviting and sophisticated atmosphere.

## Color Palette

- **Mustard Yellow**: `#f4a900` - Bold primary accent
- **Terracotta**: `#c1666b` - Warm secondary color
- **Warm Beige**: `#d4b896` - Neutral backgrounds
- **Chocolate Brown**: `#4a403a` - Dark text and anchors

**Semantic role mapping**: Mustard Yellow=active/emphasis, Terracotta=context/de-emphasized, Chocolate Brown=text, Warm Beige=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSans Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans
- **If available**: Montserrat Bold for headers, Source Sans 3 for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Warm Beige `#d4b896` on Chocolate Brown `#4a403a` | 5.32:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |
| Mustard Yellow `#f4a900` on Chocolate Brown `#4a403a` | 5.04:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |

All other in-palette pairs fall below 4.5:1 — do not use them for text. No pair reaches 7:1, so for long body copy on the light Warm Beige base, darken the ink toward near-black instead of relying on Chocolate Brown alone.

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Restaurant presentations, hospitality brands, fall campaigns, cozy lifestyle content, artisan products.
