# Forest Canopy

A natural and grounded theme featuring earth tones inspired by dense forest environments.

## Color Palette

- **Forest Green**: `#2d4a2b` - Primary dark green
- **Sage**: `#7d8471` - Muted green accent
- **Olive**: `#a4ac86` - Light accent color
- **Ivory**: `#faf9f6` - Backgrounds and text

**Semantic role mapping**: Sage=active/emphasis, Olive=context/de-emphasized, Forest Green=text, Ivory=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSerif Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans
- **If available**: Lora Bold for headers, Open Sans for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Forest Green `#2d4a2b` on Ivory `#faf9f6` | 9.38:1 | AAA (≥7:1) |

This is the only in-palette pair that passes 4.5:1 — all text must use it. Sage on Ivory (3.69:1) and Forest Green on Olive (4.15:1) work for large display or graphics only.

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Environmental presentations, sustainability reports, outdoor brands, wellness content, organic products.
