# Sunset Boulevard

A warm and vibrant theme inspired by golden hour sunsets, perfect for energetic and creative presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Burnt Orange**: `#e76f51` - Primary accent color
- **Coral**: `#f4a261` - Secondary warm accent
- **Warm Sand**: `#e9c46a` - Highlighting and backgrounds
- **Deep Purple**: `#264653` - Dark contrast and text

**Semantic role mapping**: Burnt Orange=active/emphasis, Coral=context/de-emphasized, Warm Sand=text, Deep Purple=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans
- **If available**: Playfair Display Bold for headers, Lato for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Warm Sand `#e9c46a` on Deep Purple `#264653` | 6.03:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |
| Coral `#f4a261` on Deep Purple `#264653` | 4.89:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |

All other in-palette pairs fall below 4.5:1 — do not use them for text. Burnt Orange works as a graphic accent on Deep Purple (3.26:1, large display only).

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Creative pitches, marketing presentations, lifestyle brands, event promotions, inspirational content.
