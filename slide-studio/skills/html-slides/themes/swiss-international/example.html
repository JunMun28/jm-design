01 · Service levels

On-time delivery held at 96.4% while backlog fell for the third quarter

What improved

  • On-time delivery 96.4%, up from 94.1% in Q2 as the night shift stabilised.
  • Backlog down 18% to its lowest level in six quarters.
  • Expedited orders fell to 4% of volume, easing freight cost.

What still drags

  • Two hubs missed the 95% floor on inbound dock-to-stock time.
  • Returns processing remains 1.5 days slower than target.
  • Overtime hours are still above plan in the Central region.
