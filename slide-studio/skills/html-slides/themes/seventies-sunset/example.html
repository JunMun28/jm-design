Sundial Goods · annual letter 02
Chapter one

Three vows we kept

Repair before replace

Every product opens with one tool. We mailed 1,142 repair kits this year — and sold 1,141 fewer replacements.

One launch a year

We said no to nine good ideas so that one great one could take its time in the workshop.

Price the decade

We cost our goods over ten years of use, not one season of fashion.
