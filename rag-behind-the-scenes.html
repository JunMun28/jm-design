Accurate, trustworthy, and source-aware response

Documents are split into small chunks, converted to embeddings, and stored for efficient retrieval.

The system searches the vector database to find semantically relevant information.

Only the most relevant information is passed to the model to keep answers focused and accurate.

The LLM uses the provided context to generate a natural, accurate answer.

Users get answers they can trust, with the ability to trace back to the sources.
