# Desert Rose

A soft and sophisticated theme with dusty, muted tones perfect for elegant presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Dusty Rose**: `#d4a5a5` - Soft primary color
- **Clay**: `#b87d6d` - Earthy accent
- **Sand**: `#e8d5c4` - Warm neutral backgrounds
- **Deep Burgundy**: `#5d2e46` - Rich dark contrast

**Semantic role mapping**: Clay=active/emphasis, Dusty Rose=context/de-emphasized, Deep Burgundy=text, Sand=base/background

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSans Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans
- **If available**: EB Garamond Bold for headers, Lato for body (keep the default pair above for PDF rendering)

## Approved Pairs (WCAG-checked)

Ratios are direction-independent — either color in a pair may be the foreground.

| Pair | Ratio | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Deep Burgundy `#5d2e46` on Sand `#e8d5c4` | 7.58:1 | AAA (≥7:1) |
| Dusty Rose `#d4a5a5` on Deep Burgundy `#5d2e46` | 5.00:1 | AA (≥4.5:1) |

All other in-palette pairs fall below 4.5:1 — do not use them for text. Clay on Deep Burgundy (3.18:1) works for large display only.

## Type Scale

- **Display**: 60px
- **Heading**: 36px
- **Body**: 24px
- **Caption**: 20px minimum

Matches the html-slides font-size floors.

## Best Used For

Fashion presentations, beauty brands, wedding planning, interior design, boutique businesses.
