Method04

Working grammar

Three rules hold the room.

I

Make the invitation explicit.

People contribute better when entry points are named.

II

Keep decisions inspectable.

Show what changed, why it changed, and what remains open.

III

Protect the final edit.

Openness needs a clear standard for closure.
