Specimen / Glassmorphism / 2026

Lift off.

Frosted depth without the dashboard.

01 / When

SaaS pitch energy.

Use glassmorphism when the deck wants atmosphere and depth without leaning editorial. Modern lifestyle, fintech, or product showcase.

02 / Rules

One gradient. Two glass layers max. Teal as the only accent.

Blur is expensive: both for the renderer and for legibility. Stacking glass collapses the effect into mush.

End

Stay light. Stay readable.
