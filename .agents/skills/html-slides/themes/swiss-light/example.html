Section 01

Use Swiss minimal when the deck behaves like a document.

  • Research summaries with citations.
  • Technical talks read by speaker, not performed.
  • Dense agendas where structure is the point.
  • Reports whose authority comes from restraint.
Section 02

One accent per slide. The accent points at the takeaway.

If the slide has no takeaway, the accent doesn't appear. Discipline is the design; accent inflation flattens the hierarchy and erodes the theme.

→ Author intent: one focal use.

Section 03

Charts read on white. One series highlighted.

Q3 conversion rose to 4.8%, the highest reading in the trailing twelve months.

End

Restraint scales. Decoration doesn't.
